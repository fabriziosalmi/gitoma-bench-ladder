## Vulnerability Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability within this project, please follow our official disclosure process:

1. Contact the maintainers directly via [email address]
2. Provide details of the vulnerability
3. Wait for confirmation before public disclosure

We take security reports seriously and will respond within 5 business days.
