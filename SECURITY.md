## Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please follow these steps:

1. Do not disclose the vulnerability publicly or privately to anyone.
2. Contact the project maintainers through our official communication channels.
3. Provide as much detail as possible about the vulnerability, including steps to reproduce it.
4. We will acknowledge receipt of your report and work with you to verify and fix the issue.

We take security reports seriously and will respond promptly. All reports are treated confidentially until the issue is resolved.
