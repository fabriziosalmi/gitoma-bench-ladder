## Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

We take security seriously and encourage responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities. If you discover a potential security issue, please follow our vulnerability disclosure process:

1. Contact us through our official communication channels (email: security@gitoma-bench-ladder.com)
2. Provide details of the vulnerability, including steps to reproduce
3. Wait for our response before public disclosure

We will acknowledge all reports within 5 business days and provide a timeline for resolution. Thank you for helping us maintain the security of our project.
