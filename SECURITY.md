## Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

We take security seriously and encourage responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities. If you discover a potential security issue, please follow these steps:

1. **Verify the vulnerability** - Ensure it's a valid issue that affects our application.
2. **Contact us securely** - Report the vulnerability via email to security@gitoma.com.
3. **Do not publish details** - Publicly disclosing vulnerabilities without our consent is prohibited.
4. analogously, we will acknowledge receipt and provide a timeline for resolution.

Thank you for helping us maintain the security of our project!
