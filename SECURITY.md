## Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

We take security seriously and encourage responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities. If you discover a potential security issue, please follow these steps:

1. **Verify the vulnerability** - Ensure it's a genuine issue that could be exploited.
2. **Contact us securely** - Report the vulnerability via email to security@gitoma.com.
3. **Do not publish details** - Publicly disclosing vulnerabilities without our consent is prohibited.
4. 
5. **Confidentiality** - All reports are treated confidentially until a resolution is implemented.
6. **Acknowledgment** - We will acknowledge receipt of your report and provide a timeline for resolution.
7. **Reward** - We may offer a reward for verified, responsibly disclosed vulnerabilities.

For urgent matters, please contact us immediately.
